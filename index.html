Know what
you eat

Our Concept to make dining out easy!

Our goal is to make eating out easy for everyone involved. In Germany, it is mandatory to declare the 14 main allergens on products but also in restaurants. This helps people with food intolerances but the employee in restaurants need to research a lot about each of the allergens to declare it without any mistakes.

That's where easyMenu wants to help. The restaurants can scan all their products and create ingredients list for the dishes like that. The employee in the restaurants also get tips and hints for how to cook considering all allergens. easyMenu will automatically mark all allergens based on the ingredients list provided. Via our app, all customers can view the restaurants menu online including declared allergens. This makes it easier for all visitors of the restaurant to choose the best fitting dish and see an overview of the customized options.

Why Us?

Working with easyMenu means automation of menus for restaurants and customization for all visitors.

All earnings are used to work on new extensions of the app to make it even better. If you have ideas for extensions, feel free to send them to us via extension-request@easyMenu.com

Currently, we partnered with 10.000 restaurants. You can see all of them in our map overview in the app.

There are several ways to check our transparency.

1. we will upload monthly our income and how we use it to an external website.

2. if you want to check the current status of the company or other features of the product, we offer you calls to our HQ. All you have to do is to send us an email.

3. if you want to know the ingredients of specific dishes, we can provide them as long as the restaurant didn't declare it as company secret.

By using our app to generate menus including allergens, you get a lot of advantages:

The most important one being, you don't need to learn about all your ingredients - the app does that for you and tells you what allergens are included. It also provides tips what to take care of in the kitchen to make sure no allergic reaction is caused for any customer. Furthermore, you can find guides how to prevent using allergens by only small changes in the recipe.

So, as long as you stick to the ingredient list, you provided, there is no risk of declaring allergens improperly - the responsibility is fully on the app.

Another huge advantage is that the app increases attention on your restaurant and therefore gets you more customers.

Searching for dishes that match your dietary preferences ends with easyMenu. With our app, you can create a profile that saves your preferences. Based on that, the app will exclude all dishes you can't eat. This way, you get a good overview of your options.

Also, you never need to worry about where allergens are declared or whether it was done properly! No asking for special cards or adjustments in ingredients!

This app makes it not just easy to find the suitable dishes in a restaurant, it also recommends restaurants which fit your diet! Plus, it is all FREE for you.

What dietary preferences are included?

The app declares all 14 allergens: gluten, peanuts, tree nuts, celery, mustard, eggs, milk, sesame, fish, crustaceans, molluscs, soy, sulphides, lupin. Additionally, you have vegetarian and vegan options.

Josefine Mende

At the age of 17, I developed food intolerances for gluten and lactose. It was very hard adjusting to this diet. However, over time I got used to it. But one thing still stuck with me: I didn't enjoy going to restaurants. Often, I had to ask for specific menus with the allergen declaration on it or needed to get confirmation from the employee. In many cases, I encountered that the allergen declaration was wrong because the employee simply didn't know. Also, it happened that I needed to ask for adjustments of the original dish to avoid to be left with just salads. Don't get me wrong, I like salad, but if that's the only option for the meal, that's anything but appealing!

That made this project very important to me - I want to help everyone with food intolerances or dietary preferences to enjoy dining out as well as every one else to simplify the process.
