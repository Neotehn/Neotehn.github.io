How does affiliate marketing work?

In affiliate marketing, the company who wants to sell their products or increase awareness/attention for their product or service, will connect to a affiliate partner. They will advertise the company's product/service to their community. Depending on the size of the community and how trustworthy the influencer is viewed, the community will purchase the offer via the affiliate link that the influencer received from the company. For advertising the product, the affiliate partner receives a commission.

We changed this model slightly by interacting as a connector between company and their partners. We take 1% of the influencer's commission and use it to remedy shortcomings in nature. However, both the influencer and company profits from this model. Because of the green image created, they can attract more customers and therefore sell more. Influencer and the companies can also decide to share more of their income related to those affiliate links with us to increase the support and help the environment.

Why Us?

Working with Tree4Free means help saving our beautiful planet and be involved in environmental activities. Unlike with other companies, you don't have to pay additional for our service.

90% of the donated money will go to an organization of your choice, the other 10% will be used for maintenance costs of the company. Short example based on a 100€ product. The entity that advertised it would get 10€ (average Amazon Affiliateshare), from that we get 1%, that is 10 cents. Of these 10 cents, 9 cents goes to an organization and 1 cent to our company.

Our partners are established organizations in many areas. Which have been audited by us and by external institutions. If you want to make sure that your money goes to the right partners, you can communicate with us or indicate it yourself in the form.

There are several ways to check our transperency.

1. we will upload monthly our income and funds we have donated to an external website.

2. if you want to check the current status of the company or other features of the product, we offer you calls to our HQ. All you have to do is to send us an email.

3. to assure you that everything will reach the right partners, we have included contact details of each partner in the information. Upon request, they will answer all current relationships and donation status of our company.

We also have a plug-in that goes exactly the same way. If you want to get an overview of our process in advance, you can download it and try it out the next time you shop with us.

By partnering with us, your community will be impressed by the green image we help you create. This can increase your reach and draws attention to you. Also for companies, which will result in more collaborations. Of course, your network will grow and your sales increase.

With our help, you can create a green image for your company. In a time where taking care of our planet is more important then ever, these values will be appreciated by your customers and also attract new customers. Therefore, you can increase your sales as well as your analytics.

Using our browser plug-in and also buying products or services with us involved, makes it possible to profit from cheaper prices. You will be able to see an overview of our discounts for the products you are looking for when searching on Google.

How much trash is floating in the oceans?

An initial estimate of the global input of plastic waste into the oceans has calculated an amount of 4.8 to 12.7 million tons per year. This is equivalent to one truckload per minute. More recent calculations, which include lakes, rivers and oceans, assume an input of 19 to 23 million tons into these ecosystems.

How much rainforest disappears every year?

Again and again, we hear of horrifying news from the tropics that the forest has been destroyed. Every year, around 300,000 square kilometers of forest are cut down or burned worldwide - a good half of them in tropical countries: first and foremost in Brazil, the Democratic Republic of Congo and Indonesia (source: Global Forest Watch, 2017).

In 100 years without bees?

A study proves it: If things continue like this, there could be no insects left in the world in as little as 100 years. And last but not least, changes in the seasons due to climate change also have a direct impact on wild bees. As spring starts earlier each year, the flowering time of many plants also shifts. When the bees fly out, many of the plants have already faded.

What is the state of education in Africa?

One area where progress is urgently needed is African education. Less than 20 percent of African women have ever attended school. Half of the world's children who do not attend school live in Africa - that's around 30 million boys and girls.

Who are we?

Tree4Free started as a small project, motivated by six students who wanted to do something for the planet. It quickly developed into a huge success, helping companies, their affiliate partners and the environment.

What started as planting trees to reduce CO2 developed into remedying many different shortcomings in nature. It makes us proud to see the effect we have on this world and we are very thankful for all our partners - company, influencer and of course all the consumers! Without you valuing the well-being of our planet, we would not be able to do what we do.
